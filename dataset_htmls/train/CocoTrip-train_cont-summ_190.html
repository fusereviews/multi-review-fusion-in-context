We had a week at Vdara. The room was spacious and very comfortable, we were on the 36th floor and had views towards The Mandalay Bay and mountains which was nice. The kitchen area was very functional and the bathroom again was spacious. There was plenty of storage space in the room, kitchen and bathroom, also plenty of towels were supplied daily. All staff we encountered were curtious and very pleasant including housekeeping. Some have mentioned the walk to the strip, to be honest wherever you stay in Vegas you are going to have a walk due to the size of all the hotels and casinos. Would definately stay here again and I do hope to return one day as Vegas is the most amazing place.
My girls and I went for a weekend in Vegas and stayed at the Vdara. I would highly recommend this hotel! It was really spacious and very comfortable for the 5 of us. The staff was all very friendly. The hotel room has a kitchenette which is nice for bringing back leftovers or drinks. The bathroom is huge with a nice tub and a great shower. This hotel doesn't have a casino attached, but it is tram connected to the Cosmopolitan and also a couple minute walk across to the Aria. There are great restaurants close, and the whole complex is easy walking distance to the strip and many of the popular sites in Vegas. I would definitely stay here again - great place.
