I stayed here for several nights while on a tour of Italy. It is well situated in Rome, right near the subway station. There is great food just a short walk away, in almost every direction you go. The room was frequently cleaned, and well maintained. One warning, the elevator is only accessible from the second floor and up, which means that you have to carry your luggage up a flight of stairs before you can reach it! That could be difficult for some people. There is a nice terrace on the 4th or 5th floor that has some outdoor seating, and there is also some space on the roof of the building (accessible via elevator). Overall, a decent place to stay.

(Weekly unlimited ticket is only 16 euro) There is also a fantastic restaurant just around the corner, and plenty of shops in the area. The rooms are a little on the small side, but very well kept and air conditioned. I would recommend this hotel without hesitation, first class as long as you know what your getting.
