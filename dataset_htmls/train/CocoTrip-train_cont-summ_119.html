We booked Acta Mimic because it was the cheapest central hotel we could find. The hotel is situated on a back street with hardly any traffic so noise is minimal. Yes it can seem a little seedy but we had no problems and did not feel threatened. The staff were very pleasant and helpful, and spoke some English. The only thing we didn't like were the black corridors with red lights which led to the rooms. Just a little off putting. The rooms however were mostly white (black ceilings) and were spotlessly clean. Not a lot of storage but enough for a long weekend.
The Mimic is in a relatively run down area of Barcelona, but it is easy to get to the places you need to see. Although the rooms are small, ours was absolutely spotless and I am surprised to hear other reviewers saying different. The decor was different and imaginative, and added to the experience. Staff were welcoming and friendly, and the breakfast first class. We had a fabulous time in Barcelona and I would certainly stay here again. Thankyou staff!
