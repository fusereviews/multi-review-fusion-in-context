I searched long and hard to find the right hotel in Istanbul's historic Sultanahmet area, and I made the perfect choice. I was traveling with a group of 8 so the pressure was on me. The Armada's staff was friendly, attentive,and helpful, the rooms were spacious (mine had a lovely view of the Sea of Marmara), and the hotel's location was convenient. The breakfast buffet was sumptuous, and I can say honestly that the rooftop dinner we had at the Armada was the best meal we had in Istanbul, and the view of the Blue Mosque and Hagia Sophia was unbeatable. It was a 10-15 minute walk uphill to the sites, but we enjoyed the fascinating streets on the way. Faye USA
The Armada Hotel was a very good experience for me. I have seldom stayed at a hotel with such an excellent service and with such friendliness towards the customers. The hotel is situated nearly perfect within a 10 minute walk from all major attractions in Istanbul, the Blue Mosque, Hagia Sophia, Topkapi and the Grand Bazar. The view from the restaurant on the top floor was amazing, the Bosphorus in one direction and the Hagia Sophia and Blue Mosque in the other. The food was also excellent. On top of it all, the price was very modest. My stay in December was a business trip, but I intend to go back already the first week of January on a private trip.

Very enjoyable stay at Armada Hotel in Istanbul . The hotel is very well situated near the Aghia Sophia Museum ,the Topkapi Palace and the SultanAhmet Blue Mosque. The hotel staff was very pleasant and friendly. The rooms were very clean . The buffet breakfast is generous and of excellent quality. The view from the terrace restaurant is beautiful , day and night ,overlooking on one side the Marmara Sea, the Bosphorus and on the other side the Blue Mosque and Aghia Sophia.
