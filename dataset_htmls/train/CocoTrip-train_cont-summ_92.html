I have never been disappointed in a Harrah's hotel and this was no exception. The room was spacious and the bathroom was fantastic. We had a room on the 24th floor with a great view of the Mississippi River. The room was clean and since we had a king size bed there was an ample sitting area with couch, chair and table. The bed was super comfortable. The service at this hotel is top of the line. Each staff member was courteous and went out of their way to make us feel welcome and pampered. The casino is directly across the street from the hotel and the food and staff there were wonderful as well. One drawback, you have to pay for internet access. I think Harrahs should make this service part of the room fee.

If you by-pass the valet parking and self-park, you can have your parking validated after 30 minutes of casino play. We ate at the buffet and enjoyed the variety. We also dined at Besh Steaks and were very happy with our food and drink. The only negative is that the hotel doesn't have a pool. Tip - if you don't want to wait in the long lines at the Cafe du Monde for your coffee and begneits, try the stand in the Riverwalk.
