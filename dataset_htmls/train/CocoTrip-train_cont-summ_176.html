The hotel is like a boutique hotel, offering free airport pickup, a delicious free meal in their restaurant, and the staff are great! The rooms are stylish and clean(try to get a room on the back over looking the pedestrian walkway with all the little cafes), and the great breakfasts are included in the price! The location is the best part! They are with in a few blocks of all the important tourist attractions, the Blue Mosque,Haggia Sofia, Topkapi Palace, the Bosphorous, and of course the Spice and Grand bazaars! The best part of Istanbul is right in this area! Explore and enjoy! G and A
