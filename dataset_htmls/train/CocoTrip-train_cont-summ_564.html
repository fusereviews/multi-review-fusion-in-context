We stayed at this hotel for 6 nights last year. The hotel is very clean and the staff are very helpful, curteous and pleasant. Our room faced rue de l"opera so it could be noisy some nights, particularly on the weekend but, in fairness, we live in a small city. On the plus side, it is very centrally located and within walking distance to the area where the tour buses depart and arrive and quite close to the Louvre. One tip.unless you have endless money to blow, skip the buffet breakfast; it is a bit of a rip off when compared to buffet's that we have purchased in other European hotels. If you have the time, take the day trips to Bruge and to Versailles. You will not regret it.
We have stayed at this hotel on a number of occasions, and have always found the hotel itself to be perfect. The location is excellent, and the rooms are scrupulously clean, spacious and elegant. The hotel staff, i.e. front desk, concierge etc are amazing, and always go out of their way to be helpful. Sadly, when it comes to the restaurant it is like another world. Whilst the food at breakfast is perfectly adequate, the staff are not welcoming and very distant. At night the food in the restaurant is nowhere near as good as other restaurants in the vicinity. Like a pevious reviewer I hope Hotel Management reads this review as the restaurant and its staff are distracting from what is otherwise a faultless hotel
