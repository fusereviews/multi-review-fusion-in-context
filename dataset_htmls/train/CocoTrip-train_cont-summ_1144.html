We wanted to stay in the Marais, and I found this hotel on the internet. We were extremely happy with our stay, and will return here again. The hotel is spotless. While our room was a bit small, it had a wood beam running through the ceiling which lent it that Parisian ambience. And the bathroom was spacious with a wonderful tub for bubble baths. The breakfast was huge with many choices. A metro stop is kitty-corner across the street, and the neighborhood is very nice with grocery stores and cafes within a block of the hotel. Sam at the front desk was so nice, and gave me special flower honey for my sore throat.thanks, Sam. I'll be back!

Summary

The hotel was fine for the price. It definitely was not a glamorous hotel. The staff was nice and there at all hours of the day and night. They were helpful with directions around the city but not very helpful booking excursions. The room had an odor but was relatively clean. The bathroom was small but had what you needed. The hair dryer was a bit weak. The shower pressure was acceptable. The bed was comfortable. The hotel is not in the greatest area.
