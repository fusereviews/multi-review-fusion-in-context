Summary

Very pleased with the the Ocean Mecca hotel. It was in a great location - on the boardwalk but away from all the loud noise and crowds. Everything was within walking distance - restaurants, mini-golf, stores, rides, etc. The beach in front of the hotel was very nice and the pool was great. The staff were all very friendly and the room was clean. Only issue is that there is no refrigerator in the room . I highly recommend this hotel to anyone who is looking to spend a nice relaxing vacation in ocean city!
