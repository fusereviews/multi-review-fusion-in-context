We stayed here for 7 nights and found the Excalibur to be a very good hotel. We stayed in tower 2 and our room was very spacious and very comfortable. The hotel itself is very busy and has a lot of amenities. The buffet breakfast was great, we did not try lunch or dinner. The coffee bar at the entrance to tower 2 was fantastic and is open 24 hours. The Excalibur is located quite far down the south of the strip, it is very close to New York New York, Luxor, MGM and the Mandelay Bay. There is a tram or a moving walkway from the Excalibur that will take you to the luxor and the Mandelay Bay.
We stayed at the Excalibur for 3 nights just after Labor Day. The rooms were average but very clean and comfortable. The first night we ate at the Round Table buffet, which was good as far as buffets go. You won't go away hungry. The second night we went to the "Tournment of Kings.'' It was very entertaining and the food was pretty good. The third night we ate at the Sherwood Cafe. The food was very good and lots of it for the money. We had no problems during our stay. The check-in kiosks are very convenient and it didn't look like too many people were taking advantage of them.
