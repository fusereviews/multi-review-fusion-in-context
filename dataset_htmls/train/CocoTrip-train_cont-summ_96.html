Summary

The Marmara Pera is a great location in the bohemian area of Beyoglu which boasts some of the best restaurants in Istanbul. The rooms are a good size and well appointed and very clean. The upper rooms at the back have a great view of the Bosphorus. The front rooms have a city view which is great for city watching. The views from the roof top bar and pool are probably the best in Istanbul. The service and staff are excellent. The hotel is conveniently located close to the metro which makes getting to the major business districts pain free and it is easy to get to the old city, Sultanahmet, by taking the historic Tunel funicular railway and the tram.
