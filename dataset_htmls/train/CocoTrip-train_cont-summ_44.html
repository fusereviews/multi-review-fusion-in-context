We stayed in a Delux room on the 7th floor with an excellent view of the Hong Kong skyline. The room & bathroom were very spacious, something which is rare for a hotel in Hong Kong. The room was clean and tidied every day. The only negative point about the rooms were the hard beds, making it a bit uncomfortable for someone who prefers a softer bed. The hotel is located 15 minutes walk from MTR Tsim Sha Tsui, which was ok. Along the way to the MTR station you can find plenty of places to have your breakfast (Delifrance, Subway, Starbucks). The location is very central and convenient - around Tsim Sha Tsui you can find large shopping malls with excellent restaurants. We had a great stay!
Nikko Hotel is in Kowloon, near to the harbour. You need to walk a bit to get to the main shopping district of Tsim Sha Tsui and you need to cross the harbour to get to Hong Kong Island proper. There are plenty of restaurants near the hotel and some rooms have a good view of the harbour. The Nikko Floor Lounge has a good selection of drinks and ice cream, although it is a little cramped. The staff are mainly very attentive and responsive. The room is fairly standard. Comfortable. Nothing to complain about. Breakfast choices at the Cafe Serena is not that wide but lunch buffet is good. Overall, a decent place to stay. Nothing much to complain about.
