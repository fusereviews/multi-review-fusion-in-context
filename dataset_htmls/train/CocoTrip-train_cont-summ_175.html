If you want to be near the airport, I recommend this hotel! Nice location within a few miles of MCO. We stayed here overnight for an early flight out in the morning. The hotel is new and in great condition. The suites are nice with a small seating area and nice comfortable bedroom. I really liked the lights directly over the headboard; they were controlled by a switch close by-great for reading! Small sink but nice shelves around it. We had a view of a small lake and the outdoor pool from our window. Breakfast was good with a few hot items along with the usual fare. I hope to stay here again when we fly in and out of Orlando.

And the other part of the room is the bed and bathroom. There isn't a lot of counter space, but still very nice. Complimentary breakfast is served and the spread is the typical continental breakfast - bread, muffins, waffles, eggs, sausage, yogurt, etc. It's very close to the airport and the Hertz rental car returns. You can walk to nearby restaurants too.

Summary

It is located very close to the airport and very convenient for a rental car return at the airport. There is also an airport shuttle. The rooms were comfortable and the breakfast was good for this price range. The staff was friendly and this place is a good value for the money. The suites are nice with a bedroom, a sitting area with wet bar, and a bath area. The bathroom and shower were nice and clean! I would stay here again if in the area.
