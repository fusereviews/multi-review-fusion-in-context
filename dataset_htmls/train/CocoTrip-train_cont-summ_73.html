The AND hotel is located just around the corner of the Blue Mosque, nevertheless quiet enough. The beds are very good and the rooms are clean. I think the hotel offers good value for money: we (me and my two sons of 13 and 14 years old) payed EUR 140 per night for a 3-bed room. The only negative point: the quality of the breakfast buffet is included but only sort of ok (but good enough to survive). If I go to Istanbul again, I would certainly stay here again.

Summary

A small hotel with fantastic views of Aya Sophia and the Blue Mosque. It is across the street from the Cistern and around the corner from the Tram and many restaurants. Most of the major tourist sights are within easy walking distance and a park is being built directly in front of the hotel. The staff is wonderful and very helpful. The buffet breakfast is more than adequate in a city filled with great food. Recently, remodeled, the rooms are clean and modern. All the rooms at the front of the hotel face the Aya Sophia and some have balconies. The night-time view of the Aya Sophia is breath-taking. The location of the AND Hotel and the friendly, efficient staff are reason to return.
