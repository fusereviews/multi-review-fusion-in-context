My daughter and I stayed at the Fairfield in Key West for a weekend in February 2012. I was pleassantly surprised at how well the hotel fit in to the Key West atmosphere. I am used to Marriotts being the same everywhere, but this one had amenities you don't usually find. the pool was great, larger than normal and not the standard rectangular pool. The rooms were comfortable and clean - a bit small, but who stays in their room in Key West anyway? There were moped and electric car rentals available, and I was VERY impressed with the breakfast area! It was outside on a veranda and there was actual staff handling the service and the food. There was also a tiki bar near the pool. I would DEFINITELY go back!
My family and I stayed at the Fairfield Inn for one night last week. The pool and breakfast areas are certainly beautiful. The older rooms, which I was told were "gutted and redone", are still lacking. The door jamb was missing on the first room we were in so the noise from street traffic was unbelievable. The staff were very kind and moved us to a room toward the back. The noise from traffic was less but, the noise from the air conditioner kept us up most of the night. Try to get a suite in the new section of the hotel if booking at this location.
