Hotel Lirico was awesome. We stayed there from October 2nd to October 4th, 2007. It is very clean and very close to the Termini. Our room was a double standard - it was a small room but comfortable. The bathroom was super clean. The bed was a bit hard to sleep on. The breakfast room was nice and airy and the breakfast was awesome. All the staff were super nice and helpful. They have a website - you can contact them directly and all my emails and questions and concerns were answered the next day at the latest. I loved this place and we would definitely stay here again. I would highly recommend it!
we have just return from a 3 night stay at the lirico. we had a triple room which was a good size for the 3 of us, comfortable and very clean. we spent little time there as we were out most of the day. The hotel lift is very small and there is a flight of steps up to the reception from the street. The breakfast room was quiet small, and very busy on the second morning. The location was excellent near the railway station. Very friendly and helpful staff all spoke english very well. If like us you are looking for a decent hotel with no frills this is the one for you.
We stayed at Hotel Lirico for 4 nights using it as a base to see all the sights. The staff were friendly, rooms clean and the hotel has a very pleasant atmosphere. Our room was small and occasionally it was noisy in the morning (from around 8:30-9:00), although very quiet at night, but those were the only negatives - everything else was exactly what we needed and the location was excellent - we could walk everywhere which is what we were looking for (just 20 mins to most of the major attractions)and it's near the main railway station. We stayed at the Hotel Lirico mainly because of the great price and the convenient location. The Termini train station is about a five minute walk from the hotel. Our room was clean and small, but the staff made the stay very enjoyable. The continental breakfast in the morning was good as well. Yes, the immediate perimeter around the train station is a little shady, but the area surrounding the Lirico a few blocks away was very nice. For the price, the customer service, and the location, this hotel was a great catch.
