The hotel is in a good area and we called in advance to make certain that the room was clean for an early check-in. I was quite happy that they were able to accommodate me under short notice. The staff was nice to meet my needs. The room itself was a standard room. It is a small hotel and there was no restaurant on site. However, there are lots of places to eat close by so we had many choices. They have refrigerators in the room so we were able to keep our drinks cool. If you expect a 5 star hotel for amenities, then this is not the place. If you want a reasonable budget hotel then it will fit your needs like it did for me. I'll likely return.
