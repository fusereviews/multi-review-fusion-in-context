Close to Christmas Market We stayed here for one night in late December on our way to Ireland. I reserved two rooms on the internet and had been impressed by the hotel's helpfulness and speed and ease in replying. The front desk staff were similarly pleasant and helpful when we arrived. Our rooms were small, as were the bathromms, but well heated and very clean. The breakfast was also good and all that we required. I chose the hotel because of its proximity to the centre of the city and the Christmas market, and indeed it was perfect for this. It was bitterly cold outside and it was great to be able to get back to the hotel in a couple of minutes. We were quite happy with our experience here and would definitely recommend it to others
The Miramar Mix . A clean and comfortable hotel, with the bonus of free wireless internet for guests, and a good hearty breakfast included in the price. Very convenient for getting around the Altstadt, and not far from the cultural attractions of Frankfurt. Reasonable walking distance from the Hauptbahnhof. The staff are helpful and speak English. Like all smaller hotels in Germany the elevator is real slow (and small), at times you wonder if it's stopped, but it gets you there. The room decor is a bit startling, with more fake burr walnut panelling than you'd expect in a fake Rolls-Royce factory, and bright cheerful colours that somehow do work together but shouldn't. A good breakfast is included in the rate. I'd definitely use the Miramar again.
The best hotel breakfast I've ever had The hotel Miramar is well-located in Frankfurt, in a lovely area very close to the Altstadt. The hotel itself is not impressive looking from the outside and the reception is ridiculously small but that is the worst criticism that I can give it. The room itself was lovely, the staff were friendly and I have never had as good a breakfast as I did at the Hotel Miramar. There was something the cater for all tastes (which is not always the case in hotels on mainland Europe) and I have to say I was really set up for the day of sightseeing after it. I really enjoyed my stay there and if I were to return to Frankfurt I wouldn't dream of staying anywhere else.

Good hotel in good location This hotel is in an excellent location around the corner from the Romerberg in the centre of the old town. I stayed here for 3 nights in June 2004. The single room was compact, but spotless and the bathroom (shower only, no bath tub) looked new. It had air conditioning and was very quiet. The staff were very friendly and helpful. They had a computer set up in reception and one could purchase a voucher from the hotel to connect to the internet via the computer, and while not cheap was very convenient. I would certainly consider staying here again if I ever return to Frankfurt.

Summary

The hotel is in a good location being just around the corner from the Christmas Markets. Rooms are pretty small. While staff is very friendly and helpful, there isn't really that much going for the hotel except the location. The breakfast is just adequate and the lift could barely fit one person with their luggage. The decor in the rooms is also a bit faded. All in all though, if you are looking for a reasonably priced hotel in close proximity to the old town, then this isn't a bad choice.
