Summary

Service at this Indian restaurant is very slow. Customers will often be kept waiting an excessive amount of time either to order their food, or waiting to get it after the order has been placed. The place does have solid, authentic, Indian style food at least, once it arrives. Overall this place is not recommended, due to the poor service.
