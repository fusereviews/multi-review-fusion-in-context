Stayed here for one night, to go to a music festival in Hyde Park - very good area and central location. However, we encountered a problem with our booking upon arrival, and found the staff to be unfriendly. Our room was in need of refurbishment and was quite dark. The shower was rusted, and we could not get any hot water from the taps. On the morning of our stay it rained and their was a leak from the ceiling onto our bed - nice! The breakfast wasn't up to much either. Would not stay here again, and would not recommend it until it is refurbished. If you want to stay in this area, I would recommend the Hyde Park Radnor Hotel just around the corner - excellent, but book early.
