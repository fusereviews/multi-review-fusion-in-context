Not a 4-star hotel. We stayed here as part of a group tour through northern Italy. Of the 21 rooms occupied by our group, at least 7 rooms had problems such as foul odors and non-functioning air-conditioning and the occupannts requested a room change. The first room we were given was tiny the a/c didn't work. The room they gave us next was handicap-accessible and was much larger, with a huge bathroom. The bathroom, however, had no put-down space at all we had to move a small table from the bedroom into it. The neighborhood is very seedy and we were uncomfortable walking around there at night. There are better choices in beautiful Florence.

Summary

This hotel is quite old compared to pictures on their website. Generally it's a okay hotel. The hotel is in a good location. It is close to the train station and the main attractions in Florence by foot. There are also nearby buses that go to Fiosole, the plaza Michelangelo and the discount mall. The rooms are rather small and unsafe. Some rooms are facing onto a construction site. The staff at the hotel had an unprofessional attitude towards guests. The hotel was quite noisy.
