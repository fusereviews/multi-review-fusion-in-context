We picked this hotel from the internet because of its proximatey to the Atoche Rail Station, as we were traveling on the fast train to Seville from that station. The hotel was very clean and the room was comfortable. For a large city we thought the cost was reasonable - ~$85.00 for a room with two twins. The bathroom was also very nice. The staff were friendly and helpful. If you wish to save money, the hotel can be reached from the airport by taking the metro to the Atoche Renfe Station (You change lines twice) It is then about a 5-10 minute walk from the metro stop/train station. The cost from the airport is 2 euro per person on the metro. It is also within walking distance of the Prado and Retiro Park.
Stayed in this hotel in the middle of summer for 4 nights, overall we were happy with the hotel. Things we liked: - Location - very close to all major museums and Madrid major park. Also well connected by metro to the rest of the city. The immediate area around the hotel doesnt offer much (very few shops, cafes or restaurants) but the close proximity to the city helps. - Service rooms are clean and very comfortable. Nice beds. Bathrooms are big and clean. Hotel staff are friendly. - Good value for money. Things we did not like: - Internet in the foyer too expensive and slow. - No safes in the rooms. Our stay was pleasant and comfortable, would definitely go back there.

Summary

After staying at the Best Western Atlantico on Gran Via the Agumar Hotel was in a much quieter residential neighborhood. Very close to the Prado and the Reina Sofia Museums and the Atoche Rail Station. 5 min walk. The Hotel seemed like a "tour group" hotel. It was very clean, but stark and cold. You had to pay for the use of the internet and also to use the safe. The beer and wine were very expensive. better to go down the street to a local pub. There was a restaurant there but again. expensive. Though we liked it for its proximity to the museums and the train. The staff were helpful. they had limited English.
