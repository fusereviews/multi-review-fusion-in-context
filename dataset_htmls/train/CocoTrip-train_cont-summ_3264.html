OK. It's a budget hotel so If your looking for the Crown Plaza, this is NOT the place. The stairs are the most difficult thing about The Hotel Di Ann and they are not for the weak. Otherwise, it is a great deal for the money. They provided everything we needed. The rooms are tiny and old, but the beds were comfortable, clean and warm. The bathrooms were decent and clean. The breakfast (Included with the room price) was adequate. They have a nice balcony/patio that looks over the street that is a nice place to chill. They have a nice cafe right below the hotel and many shops which will help in making your stay convenient. Euro for Euro, this was a great deal and it was everything I expected.

The beds were comfortable and clean, as was the rest of the room. We had a problem with the key card but the staff came up straight away to help us out. We found the staff in general very polite and helpful and they let us leave our luggage there on the last day. Breakfast was more than adequate. We really enjoyed our stay here and would be more than happy to stay again. It's also in a great location, really close to Dam Square and a lot of the main sights.

Our room was small, which was fine, but it wasn't very clean or well maintained. The room was on the street, which was very noisy. Also, the refrigerator didn't work.
