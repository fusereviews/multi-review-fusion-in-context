Summary

The hotel has really great location; the metro is only 2 minutes from the hotel, the Istiklal Street only 2 minutes from the hotel. There are a lot of cafes around the hotel. The service in the Hotel is very friendly, rooms are clean, comfortable and cosy. If you need non-smoking room you just need to tell this to the reception upon your check in. The breakfast is very nice but this is not 5-star hotel breakfast. There is Free Wi Fi internet in the rooms. The luggage delivery is very poor.
