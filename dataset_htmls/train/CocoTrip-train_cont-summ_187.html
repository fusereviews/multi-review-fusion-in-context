I and my wife stayed in Albergo del Senato for three nights. The hotel is a boutique hotel with a very good management in a very good location. The room was very good - quite spacious, clean, nicely decorated, with free wi-fi. The bathroom was also very good - spotless and everything worked fine. Breakfast buffet was much more than adequate. The location was superb. Our room had a nice view of the Pantheon. The hotel is in a walking distance to all of the highlights in the old part of the city. There are lots of restaurants and cafes very close to the hotel. The people at the front desk and the breakfast room was very courteous and helpful. We would certainly consider staying in Albergo del Senato in our next visit to Rome.
This hotel was the perfect spot for our honeymoon. The location was perfect; we walked everywhere while we were in Rome. The staff went above and beyond any other hotel I've been to. The front desk was always available and helpful with suggestions (especially Alessandra). The staff came to our rooms twice a day to clean up the rooms and to turn down the beds at night. The rooftop bar gave great views and the bartender Michele was very hospitable. The room was beautiful and looked right onto the Pantheon and the Piazza della Rotonda. The bathroom was spotless. And last but not least, the breakfast was amazing. There was plenty to choose from and it was all good!
We stayed at the Albergo del Senato for the first leg of our honeymoon. The hotel is in a perfect location and our room had a view of the Pantheon. Wow! The staff was usually very helpful, although upon arriving on our first day, the concierge scolded us because we didn't find the limo we had reserved at the airport (the limo driver was no where to be found). However, the other concierges during our stay were very helpful and polite. If you do get a room with a view of the square, it can be a bit noisy at night, so keep that in mind. Overall we had a wonderful stay and I would highly recommend this hotel.

Summary

The Albergo del Senato was the perfect place to stay in Rome. The bedroom and bathroom were a good size and very clean. Breakfast was very good and the wait staff was pleasant and efficient. The front desk staff was attentive and helpful if not overly friendly. You can walk everywhere from the hotel. I would definitely stay at the Albergo del Senato again and would recommend it to anyone going to Rome.
