Summary

The hotel was clean and the service at the lobby was excellent. The plus for the hotel was the location with restaurants all around, the metro on the corner and a short walk to the train that goes directly to the airports. The minus was the noise in the neighborhood in the evenings and nights. The biggest minus is for the free wifi and for the breakfast. The wifi did not work well in the evening because many were logged on, it worked fine during the morning or day. The breakfast had a very, very small selection and was NOT worth it. Overall I would propably stay there again if location was key factor but not buy breakfast and not rely on the internet connection.
