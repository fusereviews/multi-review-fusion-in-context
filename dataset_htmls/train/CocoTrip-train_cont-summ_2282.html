That was nice to have something more than cereal and muffins. We found the location to be perfect. It backs right up to the ocean and there are many shops and restaurants in either direction on the boardwalk. I didn't go to the OC to stay in my hotel room, so I was pleased. I would definitely stay here again.

My family of 5 stayed at the Comfort Inn Boardwalk - you couldn't ask for a better location! Located at the southern end of the boardwalk -close (but not too close!) to where all the shops and amusements are. Great outdoor pool - right on the boardwalk. Just a few steps from the beach. The room was very nice - 2 queen beds and a sofa bed. Nice kitchenette area (with dishes and silverware). The only complaint is the continental breakfast - small bagels, mini muffins, small danish wrapped in plastic, 2 choices of cereal and some fruit. But the rest of the place was great! I would definetely stay there again!
