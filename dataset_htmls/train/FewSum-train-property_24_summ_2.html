Summary

This is definitely more of a low-budget casino, but it has it's share of good and bad qualities. Everything is electronic. There are no dealers. The selection of games is on the small side, and a lot of them are pretty dated. The bartenders are generally great and this is one of the cheapest places to buy a beer on the strip. There's also a fantastic jukebox here. The atmosphere is fun, but not as classy as other nearby places, and kids are not permitted.
