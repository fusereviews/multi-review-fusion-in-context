The perfect choice After reading the reviews on this site we settled on this hotel, thanks to all the great reviews it got.They were all right, this hotel was perfect.The price was good , the rooms were a good size and kept spotless and the desk staff were very courteous and helpful.It was only a short walk from the train station and to all the sights in the centre.It is very close to the central market and the San Lorenzo market so you can get the freshest of food and the best of bargains as you pass by them going and coming from all the sights.If this is your first time going to Florence and you are trying to make up your mind about which hotel to stay at, choose this hotel I can guarantee you will return, I know we will.
Very good hotel for the price We spent 4 days at the Caravaggio in July 2006. The location was excellent. Close to the train station and very close of all the main attractions. We walked to visit this wonderful city and to eat in many very good restaurants close by the hotel. Our double room was in front of a small park. It was very clean and roomy. The hotel was very quiet and the employees were very helpful. We arrived early but were able to let our luggage at the reception. The hotel was just 5 minutes fron a laundrymat which was very convenient. Sole reservation is the breakfast which was ordinary. (cappucinos and hot chocolate were great though). One of the best deal we got during our 2 weeks trip.
Great value and location We stayed at the Hotel Caravaggio for two nights and it was everything I was looking for- good location, clean, and a good price. We had a room that faced the building, so it was very quiet. The room itself was very clean and the water pressure and hot water was great. The staff was very helpful- they answered all our questions and made museum reservations for us. The breakfast was more than adequate- breads, cereals, coffee, tea, juice.The location was great- a very easy to walk to pretty much all the major places in Florence.
