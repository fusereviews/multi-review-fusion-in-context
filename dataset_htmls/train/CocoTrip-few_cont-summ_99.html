Also, we weren't very fond of the bed. It was a bit on the hard side and wasn't exactly plus. It wasn't horrible though. Again, not horrible, but our room was very hot no matter how we set the temp at.

I stayed here for two nights to partake in the Chicago St. Patricks festivities. Check in went super fast, and we were given a room the was so newly remodeled you could still catch a whiff of the fresh paint. It overlooked the river, which made for some great shots when they dyed it! Also, they allowed us a late check out of 2pm, at no charge, in order to not miss the parade. The beds were amazing, as was the bathroom. Plenty of space, with a very nice desk set up. And being one block from Michigan, and a 10 minute walk to Navy Pier, this is a great location. Couldn't have been more pleased with this hotel.
We loved our stay at the Hyatt Regency. I really don't think there is a better location in downtown. So conveniently located off the river and a short jaunt to Michigan Ave, Grant Park, and Navy Pier. I have already recommended this hotel to other travelers heading to Chicago. I would say, however, the dining in the hotel is pretty pricey. So, if you are looking to save a few bucks get outside of the four walls of the hotel. That being said, I will still always stay at this hotel when traveling to Chicago, with out a doubt.

Summary

This hotel is great and couldn't be better, therefore it is definitely recommended especially to other travellers heading to Chicago. The location of this hotel is unbeatable because it is just off of the river. There is an easy walk to experience downtown as well so that's a great bonus to this well-located hotel. The rooms were spacious but quite dusty and hot. The beds were adequate and the bathrooms were amazing. Some of the cleaning staff did miss an area that should have been cleaned. Unbelievably it was quite expensive just for a canned soft-drink from the minibar in this hotel room. The only available parking at the hotel is valet which can be costly. The use of internet is costly too.
