Summary

This Indian restaurant is known for serving generally good food and their biriyani dishes are especially popular. The service itself is hit or miss, and there is often a long wait for food. There have been complaints that the staff are not LGBT-friendly. Overall this restaurant is popular in the community and has tasty food, but the service is unreliable.
