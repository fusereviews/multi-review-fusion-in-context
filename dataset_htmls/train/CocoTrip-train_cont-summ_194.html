We stayed for 3 nights recently. The location is good, very short walk to the boardwalk and beach. It's close enough to walk to the main busy part of the boardwalk but far enough away to be quiet. The outside is a little dated but everything was clean. Our 5th floor room had a nice view of both the ocean and the bay. The balcony was a good size as well. We fit 4 adults comfortably for our whole stay. Remember to ask for sheets for the pullout sofa if you are going to use it as they were not in the room. We also only had 1 clothes hanger. The staff was easy to deal with but had lost my paperwork so there was a little wait at check-in. I would probably stay here again.
My husband, daughter and I stayed at The Spinnaker last week and it was great! I am really picky and I can't find one thing negative to say. The view was great, just steps to the boardwalk, the beds were really comfortable and so were the pillows, the housekeeping was great (Alice), the room was big and the kitchen was more than adequate. The staff in the office were friendly and efficient. I asked for an extra chair for the balcony and it was there before I even hung up the phone. We didn't use the pool but it looked clean and there is a huge sundeck. Most of all, the place was CLEAN! We can't wait to go back next year.
