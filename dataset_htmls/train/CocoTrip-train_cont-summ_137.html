Good place to stay We stayed at the Galeria Plaza for the Thanksgiving break. The hotel location is in the business and night life district, Zona Rosa and felt very secure. The hotel is not in a tourist area but in clean, upmarket sorroundings and only 2 mins walk to the Tourist bus stop. The rooms were as expected for a upper class hotel and very comfortable. The staff were excellent. We used the services of the very helpful concierge in organizing guides and sightseeing in and around Mexico city. The restaurant at the hotel was OK although there is a lot of choice of good eating places in the neighbouring streets. Minor quibble-to get the free internet, one had to use the business centre or the lobby. All in all, a comfortable and satisfying stay.
Nice, affordable hotel This is a great hotel with a very convenient location. Although the beds are as hard as a rock, the staff are very helpful, most speak fluent English, and the location and price ($135 average a night) are hard to beat. The modern, dark chocolate decorating scheme is a nice touch, and the water here is purified - which definitely matters and helps keep the cost of all those bottles of agua purificado down. The hotel gouges guests on the little things ($3.30 for 15 mins on a computer with internet, $28 for a five minute cab ride to the Ballet and back), but in the end, its convenience, service and overall affordable room rates make it a worthwhile choice. ~B
Modern, well appointed and quiet The room was a delight-attractive, light- and soundproof. Located in the Pink Zone not far from Chapultapec Park, the Galeria Plaza staff is helpful, cooperative and friendly. The hotel has a strong commitment to green hospitality. The card key unlocks the door and, inside, provides access to lighting. When the card is removed as you exit the room, the lights go off. The hotel gives you the option of reusing your towels to reduce energy and water use. (How often do you change your towels at home?)Except for the $8 shots at the bar, I had no complaints about my four-day stay and would recommend the hotel to anyone not needing to be downtown.
