We stayed in this hotel for three nights. We arrived and the friendly young man at the desk checked us in and took us to our rooms which were cosy, very clean and both had ensuite bathrooms. The continental buffet breakfast, it was good, adequate for the morning start. Most importantly the place was very clean, something you can't take for granted. Great location, very close to Paddington station and Lancaster gate which made it easy for us to go shopping and sightseeing. There are also a lot of restaurants and pubs in the neighbourhood, so you have a lot of choice. All in All, hotel is good value for money and we will be back.
