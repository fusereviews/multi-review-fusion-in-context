Summary

Wonderful hotel. The location is perfect - you are at the starting point of the ever exuberent Istiklal Street and the buzzing Taksim Square. The metro station is right in front of the hotel and all the transport options are easily available. The hotel is a delight to stay in - very comfortable rooms of more than the avearge size in Istanbul hotels. The only issue I have is that the mattress was a bit too soft. The breakfast was good enough by the European standards. The view from the rooms is great, on all 4 sides. Strongly recommended that should you wish to stay in the new Istanbul area, this is the hotel to be in.
