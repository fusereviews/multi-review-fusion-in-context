The hotel Eiffel Seine is lovely. The location just opposite the metro and being able to walk to the eiffel Tower was extremely convenient. The rooms are smallish but adequate and the bathrooms are great. The staff are gracious and happy to be of assistance. Breakfast was included in the price and there was a wide variety to choose from. Part of the attraction was the small lift as after a day touring, stairs just weren't an option. We also made use of the free internet which was great. The small fridge in the room also enabled us to get some drinks and snacks from the supermarket to take on our travels. Overall we were very impressed with the Eiffel Seine and would most definitely stay there again and I wouldn't hesitate to recommend it to anyone.
I would happily recommend this hotel to others. The hotel was in an ideal location, just a minutes walk to the nearest metro station and 5 minutes walk to the eiffel tower. The hotel staff are all really friendly and helpful. The rooms are comfortable and clean but are quite small, however the location and the price of the hotel more than makes up for this. The only qualm I did have with the hotel was that the rooms were far to hot at night, however considering it was minus 10 outside others may have appreciated this. I would not hesistate to rate our overall experience as excellent and would advise anyone who is after a clean, comfortable hotel, in the heart of Paris and at a reasonable price to give the Eiffel Seine a go
