Reviews

Good for what we needed Because we are a bi-racial couple, we were hesitant to book this hotel due to a negative review re. the hotel's service to minorities. But we took a chance and were happy we did. The people at the hotel were actually very accomodating and even offered to talk to the pizza-delivery place for us when we ordered dinner by phone. (Neither of us speak Spanish too well.)The location is pretty far from Barcelona proper, so we would recommend this hotel only if you need to stay close to the airport. Also, the shuttle picked us up from the airport much later than we thought it would. We would've given a higher rating if the shuttle service to the hotel was better.
Great Airport Hotel This hotel is a great hotel for the airport. There is a free shuttle which picks you up or drops you off from the airport which is about 5 minutes drive away.The hotel itself was modern and clean, the bed was v comfortable and the bathroom spacious. It is also convenient to get to the centre of Barcelona too from this hotel, you will need to walk 5 minutes and you will get to a train station which you can take to the centre in about 15 minutes. The trains are also quite frequent - every 15 minutes or so and also run until quite late.We didn't try the other facilities they had at the hotel like the gym or pool or jucuzzi unfortunately. I would definitely recommend this hotel.
Great hotel if you want to be near the Airport If you need to be near the Barcelona airport then this is the hotel. Upon our arrival to the airport we called for a free shuttle. It arrived within 15-20 minutes. The pickup point is outside between terminals BC and has a sign listing the hotels serviced. The hotel itself was very clean and quiet and the room was a good size. The fitness room was really small but fit our needs. We skipped breakfast and went down the street. Like another reviewer wrote, turn right outside the door and go down a couple blocks. On the right side is a good bakery with coffee too. Overall, a great deal and the service staff were excellent.
Good overnight, would stay again This hotel is relatively close to the airport and they do have a free shuttle in the morning that you book the day before. It is however quite far away from the shopping and sites of Barcelona. We walked the 10mins to the train station and were in the action in about 30mins. Rooms were clean, antique furniture was neat. Pool area was enclosed so very quiet and sauna was hot. We left to early to hit the breakfast. For an overnight this was great but if your in Barcelona for more than a day or so, you would probably want accomodations closer to the tourist areas.
Good Choice We chose this hotel as it was near to the airport with a free shuttle as we were picking up a hire car at the airport 2 days later. We used the hotel shuttle bus which arrived in a few minutes. We found this hotel excellent value for money, the rooms were of a good standard and the breakfast more than adequate. We used the train to get to the centre which was a 15mins walk from the hotel. If I had been staying more than 2 nights I would have booked a hotel in the centre to save the train journey back and forwards but the hotel itself I couldn't fault.

Summary

Avoid this hotel at all cost! The physical infrastructure of the hotel was great i.e room etc but the customer service for ethnic minority client was far from acceptable. The staff was very unhelpful and rude each time we rang or went to the front desk to enquire about anything. The bar was closed when we tried to get stuff to drink and order food. It was very obvious the service we recieved was very different from the caucasian clients at the hotel. We were not even informed or offer the free shuttle to the airport as the others were. I highly recommend all ethnic minorities avoid this hotel.
