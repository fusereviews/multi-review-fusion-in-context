We stayed here for four night at the end of August. The rooms were good, and cleaned every day to a high standard. The staff were nice. The lift to get up to the hotel was small and sometimes took ages to come and it closed at midnight in which case you'd have to walk up a flight of outdoor stairs, not great late at night and in the heat! What was great about this hotel was the location. Our bus from the airport dropped us straight across the road and you are on top of the train and tube station so anywhere is accessible from here. Some lovely cafes, shops and resturants in the little streets surrounding it which definately deserve a try.
It was quite simple really! We arrived by air and took the main Airport Bus straight to the front of the hotel. The hotel was clean, in great condition, cool and the rooms comfortable and modern. The location was excellent as it was above the main train station with metro routes available right throughout the city. The hotel had its own lift from the station, which was open till 11pm. Once this was shut then it was quite easy to climb the well-lit stairs on the side of the hotel. I am sure there are more quaint hotels with a central location but this one was excellent quality, price and with Taxi's and metro trains was accessible and a great hideaway!

We stayed at the Barcelo Sants for 4 nights in March 2005 and found the hotel to live up to our expectations. The location is great as it is located right above the train, bus and metro stations. Service to the airport, the rest of Spain (and even France) and elsewhere in the city are fast and practical. The hotel has its own entrance directly in the station. Also, for those who do not want to eat at the hotel restaurant, the station has several restaurants which are not expensive. The room was big enough, clean, and the service impeccable. We would return.

Summary

I found the hotel to be great - the staff were friendly and willing to accomodate requests such as early check in. The rooms were spacious and very clean, the lobby area was impressive and the facilities in the hotel were of good standard. The location of the hotel was great for accessing metro and trains as well as being on the red route of the hop on hop off tour bus. The only down side of the hotel was the entrance at night when the street easy access was closed - to get to the front of the hotel meant climbing lots of steps and walking through a deserted car park. However, I would recommend the hotel and return to it.
