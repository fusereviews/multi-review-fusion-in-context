The Courtyard isn't exactly in the quietest part of town (you can hear take offs and landings during the day since it's very close to the runway). After the airport quiets down, it's a nice relaxing beachfront hotel. The casino is small but enough to get your slot machine or blackjack fix in. A wide range of restaurants is in the lobby to serve you. Best of all, the staff is incredibly accomodating, helpful and nice 24/7. The pool/beach attendants are great and the rum drinks are yummy (we even got coupons for 2 free ones). Bed is comfortable, bottled water in the room is a nice touch. All-in-all, a peaceful stay.
This is one of three hotels that I stayed at in this area and it is by far my favorite! The check in was smooth. The ocean front view was amazing. The common areas were bustling but not too crowded. The food was decent but a bit over priced like at any resort. The casino was nice but small. The free arcade was nice as well. The beach was great! Not overly crowded and the staff was attentive. And unlike other hotels, you walk right out the back entrance to the beach instead of having to go through a maze and a gate. Would definitely stay here again if in the area and would recommend to others

Summary

This is a clean hotel with a great beach and pool area. The free breakfast buffett is a real plus. The rooms are comfortable and most have a great view of the Ocean. The service was very good. Isla Verde is very close to the airport. The courtyard is at the end of the Isla Verde "strip", so it is a bit of a walk to get to the other hotels or shopping and restaurants. The only other negative things I noticed were the price of the drinks, and 6 dollars for a beach chair rental. The chairs at the pool are free, and the beach is right there. You can easily go from pool to beach. Very enjoyable stay.
