The location is great - one can actually see the hotel from the intersection outside of the Termini. I stayed 4 nights in total - 2 nights on a Friday and Saturday and 2 nights at the end of the following week on Thursday and Friday. The room for the first 2 nights was unexpectedly spacious with high ceilings. Not what I expect for a Europe hotel. The bed was very big. The bathroom was lush with marble floors. The room for the last 2 nights were smaller than the first one despite at a more expensive rate. The bed was the same size as the bigger room. Overall, this is a modern hotel at a very good price. The rooms are spacious and clean, and the location is excellent.

Summary

This hotel is situated near the Roma Termini Railway station, so its a good location if you need to take the train in the morning. The hotel is situated on a very busy street, so unless you get a room at the back of the hotel or on a high floor, you will be disturbed by the traffic all through the night. The hotel is definitely NOT cosy and gives a sterile and cold impression. The room was clean and the bed was OK but it was very dark. Additionally, there was a strange odor of perfume in the room. All in all, we will not return to this hotel and one night was enough.
