The metro is not far off either. I would recommend that one takes rooms ont he higher levels because they then offer excellent views of the golden horn. I'll definitely be staying here the next time too.
My friends and I arrived to Golden City Hotel only to realize we had incorrectly made our reservation for one week later. They were nearly full but Ali and the reservation manager Nergiz were very helpful and able to find us a room and shift our reservation. The rooms were very quiet at night, and the view of sunrise from the roof deck at breakfast was amazing. There's not much around the hotel, but it's only about 10 mins walk to the bridge over to the old city, and about the same up to Galata tower, and the beginning of lively Istikal street. The streets and alleys coming down from Galata are a little dark at night but we never felt unsafe walking home. Would stay again.
