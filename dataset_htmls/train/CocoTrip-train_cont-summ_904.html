Summary

The staff were helpful for the most part. The lady who worked at at the bar in the evenings was not very friendly, however. The restaurant's food was mediocre. The rooms were reasonable and a decent size for a European hotel. The worst part was the location - you had to take a bus from the hotel to the metro station and then take the metro to the city centre. It took about 35-40 minutes to get to the city centre on average depending on the wait time at the bus stop. For the price, it was ok. I would try to find a hotel closer to the metro station to avoid the bus.
