The hotel is a very typical European hotel with smaller rooms and a tiny elevator. However, the staff were extremely pleasant and the rooms were very clean. Unfortunately there is no view to speak of from the rooms but step outside and you are only steps away from the subway and blocks away from the Eiffel Tower. There was free wifi in the hotel but it was very patchy and I had to login time and time again. The bathroom was newly renovated and the shower always had hot water. All in all a very pleasant stay, if you get a chance to stay here be sure to check out Rue St. Dominique as there are plenty of restaurants, bakeries, bars and shops. Invalides is a great area and I will be open to staying at this hotel again.

We stayed here on our honeymoon in Paris in September 2004. For the price, we thought it was a good deal. Room was a decent size. The location was the best part about the hotel. There were a lot of nice restaurants on the streets surrounding, and a few good breakfast spots. The walk to the Eiffel Tower was not too long. And it was very close to the train and subway stations, which made it extremely easy to get around. The elevator was very tiny and slow, only 2 people with luggage could fit in it. But overall, the location was great, and the place was clean. I would stay here again (unless I win the lottery before our next trip to Paris :) )
Centrally located hotel in the Invalides/Tour Eiffel area close to metro and train station. The room was clean, serviced every day and probably slightly larger than average for Paris. We stayed on the 6th floor and had a small balcony overlooking the Bld del la Tour Maubourg. We didn't spend a lot of time in the hotel but front desk staff were friendly and helpful. We didn't bother with the hotel breakfast which seemed steep at 15 euro pp and instead enjoyed the offering at the "Le Recrutement" bar/cafe across the road. Plenty of shops, bars and eateries close to the hotel, and just a 10 minute walk to the Eiffel Tower. Reasonable value - we booked through a website and paid 357 euro for 3 nights.
