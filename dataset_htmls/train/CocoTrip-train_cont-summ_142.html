I recently stayed at this hotel on a three day business trip. The location was great, directly on the marina. Try to book a marina view room, the night view over the boats were outstanding. The room was clean and the bed was comfortable. It was a drive (2-3 miles) to any major restaurants or stores. The food in the hotel lobby restaurant was good, as was the service. Also, there was free WiFi in the lobby, with comfortable seating. I can see where there would be a great pre and post cruise hotel, as it is close to the LA cruise terminal. Overall I enjoyed my stay at this hotel, and would stay there again if I was in the area.

We stayed at the Doubletree, San Pedro for one night pre-cruise. We had a room with a marina view and complimentary breakfast. We enjoyed watching the activity on the marina the next day and also took a walk. It was the opening day of the yacht club. The staff here are extremely nice and helpful. It makes a big difference. We booked the shuttle for the cruise the next day for 1:30pm and were allowed to check out late with no problem. We ate dinner in the restaurant and found it pleasant and the price for our meal was reasonable. I will definitely stay here again on my next cruise as it was a very positive experience.
