really great hotel My husband and I stayed at the Hotel Privilege for 3 nights - our first visit to Florence and we came as tourists for sightseeing in Italy. The hotel and staff were exceptionally nice and the hotel was good value for money. The rooms were a good size, clean, comfortable and with aircon (very necessary). The location is perfect and an easy walk to the centre. The staff were extremely helpful and accomodating and helped us to arrange an onward travel itinerary and booked cars and hotels for us. They make an excellent breakfast here! Would recommend this place if you are looking for a good quality friendly 3 star hotel in a great location.

We were moved to an interior room the next night and all was well since we could let a breeze blow through. I would recommend this hotel to anyone in this price range.
