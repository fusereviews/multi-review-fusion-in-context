Three of us stayed in the Embassy Hotel in April'05 and it was a great hotel. Excellent location, just 10 minutes from the main train station, very close to the main tourist attractions, including the zoo. Always felt safe walking to or from the hotel at anytime. Rooms are a fair size and are clean and quiet. The breakfast is basic but nice and will get you going and you can always find an excuse to go grab more food in any of the wonderful coffee shops around Rome. Staff were also very friendly and helpful and we had no porblems leaving our bags with them on checkout so we could explore Rome further while waiting to go to the airport later in the day. A very satisfying trip and I would stay there again without hesitation.

Just returned from Rome where I spent 3nights at the Hotel Embassy in a triple room with my 2 daughters one 24 and the other 14. The hotel is situated in a street about 10minutes walk from the top of the Via Veneto and is in a building shared with other hotels and offices. I found the staff to be pleasant and helpful and our room and bathroom were both clean and modern with clean towels etc each day. The breakfast was a continental breakfast e.g yoghurt, juice, croissants, coffee etc and was enough for us. I thought this was a good value hotel not too far from the main attractions of Rome and we never felt unsafe in the area at all.

Staff was more than polite and eager to help with all questions and necessary information to find our way through the city. Breakfast was delicious and much more than "continental breakfast" as described in their website. I truly enjoyed the stay in Embassy hotel and I strongly recommend this place.
