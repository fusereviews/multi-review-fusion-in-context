I normally stay at the JW Marriott across the street when on business in Miami, but decided a change would be good and chose the Conrad. The staff were polite and professional, but I found the elevator system to be annoying (the hotel is on the 25th floor, and you have to go around two corners and down a corridor to access the guest room elevators). The reception desk advised that I could self-park across the street, however upon checking out the parking garage told me that they didn't do this any more and any cars left overnight would now be towed! The room was clean, the amenities satisfactory, but I prefer the straight-forward layout and normal parking set-up of the JW, which is the same price.

Stayed here 1 night in April. I got upgraded to a 1 bedroom suite which was probably the nicest hotel room I've ever stayed in. Everything was immaculate. The view, the bathroom, the bed, everything. Checkin was a little odd, being on the 25th floor. My room was on the 21st, so I had to take an elevator up to 25, walk around a corner, then another elevator down to 21. No big deal considering what I had for a room. Great telescope to look out at the water or the city. Bring your ipod for the in room ipod stereo. The room service was also top notch. A+ stay.
I've stayed at the Conrad in Miami several times. Sometimes it is really good, sometimes (like last week) it was just OK. The rooms are good, clean, and well appointed. But some of the rooms are great (like the one I had two trips ago) and others are OK. My bathroom on this trip had a great view! The bar on the 25th floor is good (service and food/wine options aren't great) but it's nice to be able to sit outside. Atrio (the nice restaurant on the 25th floor) is lovely - I ate there for the first time this trip and will eat there again for sure.
