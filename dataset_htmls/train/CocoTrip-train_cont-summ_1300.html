This was our fourth stay at Le Hotel Clement, and we will return. The location is perfect for us, the rooms are very comfortable, clean, and cute with air conditioning ,and every room has a shower (hand-held) in the tub. There is very little help or recognition from the front desk, but they are willing to answer simple questions. There are many, many good and inexpensive (for Paris) restaurants in the immediate area, all within a short walking distance. Even th;e rooms facing the street are quiet, it's not a heavily trafficked street. The rooms are not large but are more than adequate in size. We have stayed in many hotels in Paris, but for price and location the Hotel Le Clement can't be beat.
We highly recommend the Hotel Le Clement on Rue Clement, in the heart of Paris in the St. Germain Des Pres district. We could not have asked for a better location - so close to all of the great restaurants and shops in the St. Germain Des Pres. A short walk to the famous cafes: Cafe Flore, Le Deux Magots, etc. Plus, it's only one block south of Boulevard St, Germain and four blocks south of the River Seine and the Louvre. The neighborhood is very safe too. My wife and I spent our 10 day honeymoon here and were impressed at how friendly and dedicated the staff was. Our Room was clean and had all of the comforts to make our stay very pleasant. We would definitely stay at Hotel Le Clement again.

Summary

Hotel Le Clement is in a great location for sightseeing Paris. There are lots of restaurants and shops in the area. The neighborhood is safe and busy all evening. We did not have breakfast at the hotel but found good places nearby. The rooms were very small but clean. The airconditioning was controlled by the front desk and managed to cool the room. If the price seems right I might stay here again for the location. There was really nothing wrong with the hotel, but then nothing special about it either.
