I've been in this hotel for 3 nights on the beginning of June 2010 with my girlfriend. The room was small, as almost everyone in London but it was what we expected. Small, but clean and suficient for everything we needed. There was some noise during the night because our room was next to the elevator. We heard lot of noises coming from the floor of the room upstairs. The noise was really what we disliked more. The best was the location. We could go by foot to 2 metro stations (victoria and pimlico) and we could go by foot to central places like big ben or buckingham palace (20 minute walk). Belgrave road is full of hotels and it's a very good place to start your trip in London.
