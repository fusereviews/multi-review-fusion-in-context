Ok and has a pool The hotel is located just opposite the central station which is very handy but not very scenic and there is a some background noise. We didn't find any good restaurants/bars nearby and you are near a number of seedy sex shops. The hotel is reasonable though for the price we paid. The rooms are a good size and comfortable. The breakfast buffet is really good with lots of choice and there is fruit and cake in the reception throughout the day. The reception staff were really helpful and were able to answer all our questions. There is a small swimming pool, sauna and steam room at the top of the hotel which overlooks the bright lights of the Frankfurt skyline - perfect if it's raining outside.

Summary

Great location, hotel could have been better. We stayed in the Savoy. The hotel is in a great location, 2 minutes walk from the train station and an easy stroll to all the sites. The reception looked nice and the staff were good. However, we were disappointed with our room, firstly the interior was quite shabby and the shower leaked all over the bathroom. However, it wasn't bad for the money we paid so if you can get the hotel for a cheap price it is fine, but don't pay a lot.
