Pleasantly Surprised. We were a bit concerned about staying at the Marseilles after reading some of the reviews on the internet but I have to say we were more than pleasantly surprised. The front desk staff couldn't have been friendlier and our room was much better than we had anticipated. It was spacious and clean. The bar area is small but, again, the bartender was great. The pool area was attractive and, contrary to other reviews, the pool was very clean. The location is worth a much higher rate with the best hotels on the beach within a block of the Marseilles. All area attractions are within a very short walk. The Marseilles is a work in progress with renovations ongoing. I look forward to staying there on my next visit to Miami.

Good value hotel for the district Originally booked a cheaper hotel, then came across this website so decided to cancel the earlier booking and re-book the Marseille. An excellent decision. The hotel was very clean and had all the required facilities. We had standard rooms, no great views but we don't spend time staring out the window! We stayed for 2 nights (2-3 Aug) at $82 (45) per night incl taxes. Parking was available at $12 per day just round the corner. The rooms were not large, but everything worked. The location was great for the beach and all that South Beach has to offer.
