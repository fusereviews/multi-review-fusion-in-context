My wife and I went to Rome with our 3-year-old daughter, around Easter hoilday. We stayed in the hotel Romulus. We booked the whole trip with lastminute.com. There is a good bus service: it takes about 25 minutes to get to Rome. The bus is regular, about every 30 minutes. The hotel staff is pleasant and friendly. The hotel is clean. There is an option to have an American or an Italian breakfast. In order to travel around Rome you can buy a travel card (metro+bus+tram), which is 4 euros for one day or 16 euros for 7 days. The bus final stop is at Tiburtina station, which has a left-luggage office and a train connection to Fiumicino Airport.
