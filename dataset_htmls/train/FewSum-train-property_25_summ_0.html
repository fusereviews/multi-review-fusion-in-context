Summary

This restaurant offers large portions of delicious, flavorful foods at cheap prices. The curries are the main attraction, but there is of course the tikka masala, soups, kufta kebab, naan, etc. all with a hot bar/buffet. The service makes sure that a wonderful experience is to be had.
