Summary

The room was cleaned every day and the bed was so comfortable. Excellent location. Went to see the dolphins and the white tigers, we got a discount for this as we were staying in the hotel. Didn't like having to walk through the smokey casino, but this was the same in every hotel we visited. The buffet restaurant was our favourite, didn't really like the others. The pool was a big disappointment, the sun loungers were packed in like sardines, there was no room to move whatsoever. All the hotels are amazing and our holiday was fantastic but we would not stay in the Mirage again.
