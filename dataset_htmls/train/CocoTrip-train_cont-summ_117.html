My husband and I booked the Monte Carlo for a weekend away without our two small children. We had never been to Ocean City,MD and I was nervous after reading some reviews of the Monte Carlo. We arrived early and even though the room was not ready they called our cell phone to let us know when it was. The staff was very friendly. The room was a nice size and we had a nice balcony with a side view of the ocean. The location was perfect. My husband fished on the pier while I did some shopping. We also went to two of the bars and were able to walk. We never had to move our car. We will definitley be returning next year with our children and staying at the Monte Carlo!
We just came back from a family vacation and stayed at the Hotel Monte Carlo. We've been to Ocean City a few times and this is our first time staying there. The hotel staff was very friendly and accomodating since the moment we arrived. The hotel was very clean and the room was a very nice size for the price. The boardwalk and beach were less than a minute away. The pool was very nice although there should be a bathroom up there. We didn't try the continental breakfast but we heard it wasn't much. But you couldn't beat the price considering where the hotel was located. We will definitely be going back next summer!
Our experience at the Monte Carlo Hotel was wonderful. Our stay was 8/28 - 8/30. It's a great hotel and very affordable. The staff was very helpful and pleasant. They helped with recommendation on restaurants and day activities. We were warned about parking before we got there, but parking was no problem at all. The front desk gave us a parking pass where we left our car the whole weekend with easy access to it if necessary. The location of the hotel was perfect! Just a block away from the boardwalk. I would recommend this hotel to all my family and friends.
