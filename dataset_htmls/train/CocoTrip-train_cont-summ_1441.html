If you've been travelling for a while and squeezing into compact rooms, this hotel is a welcome change. The rooms are pleasant and very large, but not air-conditioned, so with days at 30+degrees, we were lucky to be away from the west side of the building, so the heat wasn't too much of a problem and the room seemed to cool down well at night. The bathroom was of a high standard. Breakfast could get a bit frantic (as in crowded) but service was very good. The location is fantastic, and we particularly liked wandering into the leafy residential area south of Lietzenburger Str where there are some very nice restaurants. Lots of transport options were within easy walking distance and Tegel airport was a short taxi ride away.

This hotel was located about two blocks from the hotel which was very convenient. It's close to the Zoo and Aquarium. The breakfast was very good and included fresh coffee and a traditional style meat and cheese assortment along with fruit and yogurt. The hotel staff was friendly and helpful. The negatives were that the hallway we were in smelled like cigarette smoke as well as the room. We left the window open for most of the day while we were gone to bring in fresh air. The carpets were vaccuum cleaned but had stains and the sink in the bathroom leaked all over the floor. Overall the experience was average for the $$ that we spent.
