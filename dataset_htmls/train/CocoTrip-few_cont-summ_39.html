I visited Toronto end of May and stayed in this hotel with a colleague. The hotel is situated in the city centre of Toronto. The rooms in the hotel were cleaned and spacious. The bedding was very nice; the bathroom was modern with marble unit top and the toiletries were of good quality. The corridor had also an ice machine which I thought was very good idea, for those who like their cold water any time of the day. The breakfast in the hotel was also good, with a good choice of food. Newspapers were delivered to you in the morning and the staffs were very pleasant and helpful. The hotel also had a bar with a dancing floor and this was quite handy for us as we could drink and dance in. I will go there again if I ever go to Toronto and will definitely recommend it.

It is a 2 minute walk to the Eaton Centre (which is great) and about 20 -25 minute walk down to Front Street for the CN Tower.

Summary

Despite being quite a busy hotel it was really quiet. The location of this hotel is perfectly situated for exploring downtown as well as good for a medium-length walk to other attractions and sights. The hotel rooms were spacious and clean. The bathrooms were large and the room had really good bedding. The staff here at this hotel seemed a little bit uninterested but the housekeeping staff did do a really good job cleaning. The hotel had a good choice of food with a lovely breakfast and good curry lunches. The hotel has an ice machine available for guests in the room corridors.
