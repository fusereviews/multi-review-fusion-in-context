Reviews

After reading earlier reviews I was concerned that the facilities and expereince would not be as nice as I had hoped. From the description of the room we rented I thought it would be tight quarters. I was pleasantly surprised to find the atmosphere and facilities to be excellent. We stayed in a small suite, bedroom and a pull out couch. We had plenty of room and were very comfortable. The pull out couch was of excellent quality and slept better than some beds I have expereinced during travels. We stayed during the John Hagee ministeries event. The Chateau staff did an excellent job handling the large crowd staying at the hotel and those that came in for the sessions. The Chateau made a nice Spring Break destination for these Michigan travelers.

Summary

This was an okay stay here with some really lovely scenery around the place. The spa facilities at the hotel were top-class so it made for a lovely atmosphere. The room was quite noisy and didn't even have a mini-fridge inside. The pull-out couch was actually really good quality and sleep was great on it. Most of the staff were great here and provided such a memorable service. The only let-down was the doormen and front-desk lady were sometimes rude but overall the staff were great. The food was a bit expensive but the cafe restaurant definitely has unbeatable food. The hotel had a movie theatre that even played kids movies. There was a lovely lake and an indoor atrium.
