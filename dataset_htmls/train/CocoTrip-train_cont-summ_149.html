The best hotel breakfast I've ever had The hotel Miramar is well-located in Frankfurt, in a lovely area very close to the Altstadt. The hotel itself is not impressive looking from the outside and the reception is ridiculously small but that is the worst criticism that I can give it. The room itself was lovely, the staff were friendly and I have never had as good a breakfast as I did at the Hotel Miramar. There was something the cater for all tastes (which is not always the case in hotels on mainland Europe) and I have to say I was really set up for the day of sightseeing after it. I really enjoyed my stay there and if I were to return to Frankfurt I wouldn't dream of staying anywhere else.
Perfect Miramar Going to our first Christmas market in Frankfurt we wanted to make sure we made the most of the limited time we had.We had a wonderful time which was helped in no small way by having booked the Hotel Miramar. It was easy to find just 10 minutes walk from the station. The staff were most friendly and helpful (especially Mary who appeared to cover the desk in the evenings), the whole hotel was clean, efficient and the best value for money we have had on several of what we call our 'Ryanair' weekends. The location could not be better and I would have no hesitation in recommending the Hotel Miramar to anyone.
Good hotel in good location This hotel is in an excellent location around the corner from the Romerberg in the centre of the old town. I stayed here for 3 nights in June 2004. The single room was compact, but spotless and the bathroom (shower only, no bath tub) looked new. It had air conditioning and was very quiet. The staff were very friendly and helpful. They had a computer set up in reception and one could purchase a voucher from the hotel to connect to the internet via the computer, and while not cheap was very convenient. I would certainly consider staying here again if I ever return to Frankfurt.
Pleasant stay We stayed at the Miramar for two nights in December. The hotel is in a good location being just around the corner from the Christmas Markets. Our room for three was pretty small. While staff were very friendly and helpful, there wasn't really that much going for the hotel except the location. The breakfast was just adequate and the lift could barely fit one person with their luggage. The decor in the room was also a bit faded. All in all though, if you are looking for a reasonably priced hotel in close proximity to the old town, then this isn't a bad choice.
Fantastic location, clean and reasonable price I have been going to Frankfurt for the last 4 years for the Christmas markets and have stayed at this hotel for 3 of those years (we left it too late to book for the 4th time!). The hotel costs around 90 for 2 nights bed and breakfast for 2 people which is fantastic value. The hotel is 2 minutes walk from the main markets and about 4 minutes walk to the high street. The rooms are small but always clean and well looked after.
Very Good Hotel We stayed at the Miramar while visiting Frankfurt for 2 days on vacation. We found this small hotel to be very clean, quiet, and restful. The hotel receptionists were helpful and friendly and assisted us with ease. Breakfast was included and had sufficient assortment to meet most travelers needs. The Miramar was easily reached by the S line and only a few min from the main train station. Great Central location! We will definately stay at the Miramar again.
