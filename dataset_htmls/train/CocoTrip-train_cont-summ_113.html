We spent one night at Fusion on our way south. It was super easy to find from the Cancun Airport. And we were lucky enough to get off street parking right outside the hotel. There was actually a bit of confusion with our booking between emails and phone calls. But Kim was awesome and set us up with a room, and a discount. The location was perfect. RIght on the beach close to 5th, but far enough away to be quiet. The rooms were pretty basic, but you are really paying for the rest of the hotel. The room had great A/C and a comfortable bed. Hotel is on the beach with a really cool bar and great beach access. For its price range we think its a great spot!
My husband and I stayed at the Fusion the end of April, it was the second time we've stayed there. We love the service, the staff is very attentive and friendly. The food is very good, and having it served to you right on the beach is wonderful, you don't even have to leave your lounge chair! Our room was very basic, but very clean. We were in room 26, back in the garden section, and it was very quiet at night. The beach is beautiful! The location of the Fusion is perfect, right in the heart of Playa del Carmen. We just love this casual little beachfront hotel, and we would definitely recommend it for anyone looking for a reasonably priced vacation.

Stayed here with my family on a trip to Mexico and Guatemala in April 2011 and this was the hotel we loved the most at this trip. The hotel has one of the best locations in Playa if you want the closeness to the city and the beach at the same time and you will not miss a pool here. The rooms are good at the price and despite much music from the restaurant at night, this will not be a problem in the "garden view" rooms. The food here is also one of the best we tasted in Playa and the service is excellent with such nice people working here. If I'm coming back to Playa this will be my choice!

Summary

Had a great stay from the start. Kim was very nice. The room was a little outdated, yet cozy. This hotel is right on the beach, and it is a nice beach unlike in front of some of the pricier hotels. They have a free breakfast in the morning where you can eat and drink while looking out at the ocean. There was live entertainment in the restaurant the nights we were there, and the hotel is very close to 5th avenue with all the stores. This is a great hotel at a great price and we would definitely stay again!
