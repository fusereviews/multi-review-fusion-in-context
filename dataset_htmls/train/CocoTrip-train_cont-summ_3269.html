My husband and I stayed at this hotel in January, it was quite difficult to find as the signage isn't too clear, however once we found it the hotel was chic and modern. The staff were friendly and welcome glass of champagne really helped! The hotel is located around 5-10 minutes from La Rambla so is just far enough away from the hustle and noise, however there are numerous restaurants located close to the hotel. The hotel rooms are great, clean and modern with excellent facilities, the only downside to ours was being at the back of the hotel ran the metro so noise was a minor issue. The hotel's restaurant was excellent with the breakfast being the best we have ever had and the evening menu being equally good. This hotel gets a big thumbs up!
