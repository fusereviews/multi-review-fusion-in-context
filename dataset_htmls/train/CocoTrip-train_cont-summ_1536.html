Breakfast was very good, but cutlery was nowhere to be seen. Very disorganised! Rooms are OK size for two people, and comfortably appointed, but our rooms had no air conditioning. This was very disappointing as we stayed during the warm month of September. Next time I will consider something closer to the city centre.
