I was looking for a non-expensive hotel (single room) close to Termini station for 2 nights, and found Hotel Giada on the website nyceRome.com. The hotel (3 stars) is quite basic, but clean, and it satisfied my needs. The room was simply furnished, but the bed was good. The breakfast was average. The location of the hotel was superb, 3 minutes from Termini station and 3 minutes from Maria Maggiore, so I felt I got fair value for the money. There are many 3 stars hotels in the same street as this hotel, so next time i may try another one.
