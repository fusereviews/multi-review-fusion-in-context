My husband, our teenage son, our five year old son, and I stayed one night here before getting on our cruise ship. The location is excellent. We checked in and walked across the street to the Bayside Mall which had a good variety of shops, excellent restaurants, and great bars and music. This hotel is right in the center of activity, yet it was a very safe area and quiet in the hotel. We had a suite which was large and comfortable. The pool area was kind of small, but totally acceptable. It is only a short cab ride to the port. We could actually see our ship from our hotel room window. Excellent place to stay, especially if you are cruising or even if you just want to have some fun.
My family stayed at the Holiday Inn Port of Miami the night before a cruise. This hotel is in an excellent location for port travelors. It is also directly across the street from the Biscayne Marketplace with lots of shops and restaurants. The pool is very nice in the day. The pool gets a little crowded in the evening with families/kids arriving to go on cruises the next day. There is a CVS just down from the hotel and a McDonalds behind if for breakfast. Keep in mind this is downtown Miami, and there is a large homeless population. McDonalds seemed to be a popular location for the homeless. There is also security in the parking lot to protect your car, if you are driving.

Summary

This hotel is very close to the Port of Miami (ten minute or less cab ride). The hotel is right across the street from American Airlines Arena and the Bayside Marketplace, which has many shops and restaurants. I recommend this hotel if you want a place to stay for one night, maybe before or after a Cruise. The hotel is decently clean and staff was friendly, but if you want luxury this is not the place for you.
