I recently stayed at the Park Place for three days with my wife and our 6 year old daughter. The hotel was clean and well maintained. The service staff were pleasant and the front desk was helpful recommending attractions and providing directions. My daughter loved the pool and the access to the beach and boardwalk was very convenient. The room was on the top floor with an ocean view.great view of the beach, water and boardwalk, and surprisingly quiet with the balcony door shut. The hotel location is great.it is close to the amusements but not right on top of them.still right in the heart of the boardwalk with many nearby shops and restaurants. Overall, we were pleased with the hotel, the staff and the location.would definitely stay again!
