Summary

Sassy's is a favorite spot for local workers taking a lunch break. It has large portion sizes, and good variety. Some of the best items to have for lunch are sandwiches, coffee and cinnamon rolls for breakfast, and cupcakes for dessert. The staff are friendly and helpful, and the atmosphere is clean and open.
