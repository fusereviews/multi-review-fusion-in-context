My wife and I recently attended a wedding in Toronto and stayed at the Primrose Hotel as it was close to the reception. We were pleased with the service we received. We were given room 1904 which had a large king bed and sitting area. The room was clean and well decorated. The bed was extremely comfortable. I found however that the room was a little dark at night though as there were several lamps but only an overhead light by the door. The staff were very pleasant and we had no issues with them. We did not use the restaurant in the hotel but did enjoy the Tim Hortons coffee shop beside the check in area. We had a good time in the city and would use the Primrose Hotel again.
My travels landed me in Toronto and I booked in at the Primrose Best Western hotel. It is in a great location and was able to walk to some great restaurants and shopping places. The room was great, and I was given room 1705 which had a king bed and a fantastic view. It was really clean, and the housekeeping lady was very friendly and always asking if I needed any additional toiletries or coffee. The front desk staff were superb and went out of their way to help with one girl at the front desk loaning me her personal umbrella so I could go to the shops without getting soaked. The price was great, and the rooms large. This place is a definite choice when I return to the city.

We spent 5 nights at the Primrose this month. Granted the area is on the seedy side but the hotel was much better than expected. Our room was spacious and clean. Staff was friendly and efficient. The subway and public transportation was convenient and there were sufficient restaurants in the area. The biggest complaint would be the elevators. They were very slow. We happen to be in Toronto during the Grey Cup so there was lots of noise at night in the hotel. I would stay here again and would recommend it to everyone looking for a clean, conveniently located budget hotel.
