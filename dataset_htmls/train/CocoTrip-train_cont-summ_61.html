Reviews

We stayed at the Bellagio for 4 nights and had an awesome time. This hotel is right in the middle of the strip with great restaurants and entertainment both in the hotel and all around. The lobby and the conservatory were both amazing to see and the water fountains out the front of the hotel are something not to be missed. The room was huge with a great big bathroom and excellent features. We could not fault the service or anything at the hotel. The Bellagio Buffet is one of the best in Vegas but you need to get there early to avoid the line. This hotel is almost like a little city all of its own. We would definately stay here again and would recommend it to anyone.

All the restaurants have been wonderful and never encountered any member of the staff in any area of the hotel that was not nice. Wonderful, love it, would come back again and again.

Summary

Check in seemed to take ages due to the volume of people booking in and out, but after that we could not fault the hotel. All of the amenities were excellent, the rooms were clean and comfortable and the maid service was excellent. The bed was extremely comfortable and the room was of a good size as was the bathroom. The position of the hotel could not be better, right in the middle of the Strip. Service in the restaurants was attentive and the meal marvellous. The pool was of a good size, with excellent service by pool attendants and waitresses. The concierge service was first class, and very helpful. All in all probably one of the best hotels in the USA.
