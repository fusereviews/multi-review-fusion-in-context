Summary

We have stayed at the Stoneleigh in the past and enjoyed this historic hotel in the Uptown area of Dallas. However, recently we found the place disappointing. The rooms are great and the location was perfect for our visit - but this time the service made the stay less enjoyable than we expected. Maintenance issues were not addressed, the valets were difficult to locate and not always friendly, the front desk was not responsive to our complaints or knowledgeable about the area. The breakfasts at the restaurant were disappointing. The hotel seemed understaffed. There are lots of choices in Dallas and despite a great rate from hotels.com I'm sorry we chose the Stoneleigh.
