My friend and I stayed here in late march for 7 nights and loved it. The hotel is located very close to the arc de triumphe, air France bus stop and CDG Etoile metro station. The hotel staff were all very friendly and helpful. the breakfasts had the basics and was ok for 12. We stayed in a club twin room which was small but very clean. The beds were very comfortable and bathroom clean and modern (with a pretty good hair dryer). We did hear a bit of noise from the street on a few occasions as there is a cinema across the road but nothing that disturbed us too much. We also heard a tv from another night once but otherwise no noise from other rooms. I would definitely recommend it and would stay here again.

Having read all the reviews on Tripadvisor, we choose Hotel Royal Magda. The hotel is convenient for the Metro and RER line at Charles de Gaule Etoile, which is a five minute walk. You do not need to be overly energetic to walk to the Champes Elysee, and the Eiffel Tower is probably twenty minutes walk from the hotel. The hotel is small with no more than 40 rooms. The rooms are in turn small but comfortable and very clean. The hotel resides in a side street just of the main boulevard Wagram which helps reduce noise etc. The price is very reasonable and we enjoyed our stay. Recommended.
