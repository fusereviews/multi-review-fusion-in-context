Summary

This is a solid pet store with good prices and a nice variety of items including tasty treats. There's a groomer and a self-use doggie wash that get the job done well even with bigger dogs. The employees are friendly and passionate although cashiers can be a bit rude and make the customer feel rushed. Overall, a good selection of food, snacks and treats with a good although slightly inconsistent staff.
