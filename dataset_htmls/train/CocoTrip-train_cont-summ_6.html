Summary

Great Value/Location The Melasti Beach Resort was a very clean and friendly hotel. The hotel room was cleaned daily and the staff were very accomadating. The grounds of the hotel are very neat. The superior suite room was very large. The location was right on the beach and the breakfast overlooking the beach is very enjoyable. There are three large pools but not enough chairs around the paved pool area. No lawn to lay on. Other than that great place to stay for the price and location.
