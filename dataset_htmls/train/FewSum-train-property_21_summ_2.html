Summary

The sub shop isn't going to win any awards soon but has reasonably priced, decent food. The staff and customer service all depend on the time of day. Their bread to meat ratio is not close to being equal. The highlight of the menu are the fries/chips and the turkey sub. Overall the place is clean and well kept but nothing to write home about.
