We found the Chancellor hotel to a great spot for our first trip to San Francisco. We could easily access transportation, shopping, and restaurants. We also enjoyed people watching in Union Square. Our room was small, but very quiet and comfortable-we requested a room in back when we made our reservation. The view was not great, but we had no problems with street noise. There was always plenty of apples, cookies, coffee, and tea in the lobby. All of the staff members were helpful and friendly. The tours we booked picked us up and dropped us off at the hotel. It was one of our least stressful vacations.
