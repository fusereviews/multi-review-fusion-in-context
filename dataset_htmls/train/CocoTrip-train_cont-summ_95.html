Pleasant Suprise If you are traveling by train, this is a great location. Only a 5 minute walk from the station. We were a little concerned about the construction. There was not much noise when we were in the hotel, even when we had the windows opened.The room was large and lovely. Very clean. We especially liked the bathroom, with a large marble tub. The bed was very comfortable and plenty of pillows, which is sometimes a problem for those needing more than one. Breakfast was nice with a good variety of cold and hot foods. The staff was professional and friendly. We were only at this hotel for two nights, but we enjoyed our experience. Would stay again, especially if we were traveling by train again.
Great Hotel, Great Location We stayed at this hotel for 2 nights during our honeymoon - for a stop between Montepulciano and the Amalfi Coast. The location is amazing - you can walk everywhere, including the train station. We were in Florence 6 months ago and stayed at the Excelsior down by the river and this location was just as good if not better, and the hotel was half the price. The room was huge, and really nice, great amenities. Exactly what you would expect from a 4 star, but not the price. The only disappointment was that they knew it was our honeymoon and didn't acknowledge it (the hotel in Rome and on the Amalfi coast sent us champage and fruit/chocolate).
A "Charming Gem" Everything about this hotel was great! We arrived at the train station at 2 in the morning from Rome. We walked a few hundred yards to the hotel from the train station. As we walked in we were greated by name by a friendly staff person who was obviously anticipating our arrival. The room was beautifully decorated and the bathroom was cozy and spacious. We loved the bathroom shower head which had great water pressure. :-). All the shopping and restaurants were within walking distance. There is some construction going on at the church square but we did not find it to be a bother. Overall a great experience and would definately stay there again.

Great Find My wife and I just spent seven days in Florence staying at the SMN Hotel. We really enjoyed the central location and short walk to the train station. The hotel itself was wonderful with good size rooms and friendly staff. Breakfast was quite nice, served in beautifully decorated dining rooms. Unfortunately, the building beside the hotel and the plaza across the street were under construction so the view wasn't much to see. The rooms were quiet in spite of the noise from the all the constructionhowever. We would definitely recommend this hotel to anyone visting Florence.
