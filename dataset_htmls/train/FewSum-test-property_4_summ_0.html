Summary

This is trendy, upscale Chinese restaurant that sometimes hits a home run of food, but often misses the target when it comes to service. It's in a great location, clean, comfortable, and attractive on the inside. Sometimes, items on the menu are on point and offer generous portions, but other things are overly complex or drenched in too much MSG. The prices are up, and the service is often unreliable as servers can sometimes have an attitude or completely fail at being attentive.
