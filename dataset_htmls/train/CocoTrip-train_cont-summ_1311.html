My husband and I stayed at the Giotto to celebrate our birthdays in January. The hotel was better than some and no worse than others we have stayed in. Yes the rooms are small and the bathroom shower was a challenge to even the best contortionist but we only needed a room for a base as we were out every day. The bus service was cheap and regular, just down the road and the train service was excellent. The buffet style continental breakfast was adequate and set us up till lunchtime. The little cafe over the road could have been a little more adventurous but the food was cooked fresh and was good. All in all you get what you pay for and we thought it was quite good value for money.

The buffet breakfast was fine and the staff cordial and helpful. The worst part of the room was the bathroom. Not recommended.
