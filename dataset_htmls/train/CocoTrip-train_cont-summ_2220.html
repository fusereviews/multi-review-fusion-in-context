We stayed at Best Roma in October and have to say that it was wonderful. The location was ideal for walking to the main sites and with easy access to the Metro and train station and although other reviews had made us wary of the neighbourhood, we found it to be nothing but ideal. The hotel itself and our room were very well presented and breakfast was to a high standard. The best thing about staying at this hotel though was undoubtedly the staff who were clearly hard working, always pleasant and very helpful to us in getting around Rome and fully enjoying our holiday. We would most definitely stay here again and recommend it without hesitation.
