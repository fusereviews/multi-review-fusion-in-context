We stayed at the Shangri-La, Sydney in July, 2011. We received first-class service from the moment we arrived until we arrived at the airport on the day we were leaving. We had a corner room on the 27th floor, with a 260 degree view. We could see all the way from the Bridge down through the harbour, past the Opera House to the heads. We enjoyed every minute of our stay, including the food and service at both the restaurants . and even room service was special. The room itself was newly renovated so everything was clean and fresh. We did not lack for anything. The concierge was able to answer all of our questions and help us out when we needed it. It was a fabulous finale for the end of our Australian vacation.
We have stayed in a few 4 and 5 star hotels, and this one is definitely one of the best. We received a free room upgrade to get a view of Opera House and Harbour Bridge from the tenth floor. The bed was enormous, and very comfortable; and the bathroom was nearly as big as the bedroom! I had a massage at the spa while we were there, which is one of the best I'd ever had. The staff were excellent; friendly and helpful. We had valet parking and breakfast included in our room cost (good value, considering); and the breakfast was delicious. The hotel is well located in the Rocks area, with an easy walk to just about anywhere. Great place, we would definitely stay there again.
We spent some time in The Shangri La hotel as part of our honeymoon in Jan 2008 and were not disappointed. We had a deluxe harbour view room and the views of the Opera House were amazing. Our room was very modern and exceptionally clean. Reception and concierge staff were friendly and attentive. Buffet breakfast was very nice but expensive if not included in your package. The location was excellent and one 2min walk to the Rocks and 5min walk to Circular Quay, note the walk to and from the hotel is on very, very steep hills. My only complaint would be the Spa, the treatments were expensive and the reception staff of the spa, I found very unhelpful. The hotel is very expensive but as a treat, worth it!

This is the second time we have stayed at a Shangri La property (Bangkok was the first) and once again the service was very good. Nice, welcoming people both at check in and at the concierge. Free access to the internet and plenty of computers available. Sydney is an expensive city but this hotel is a decent value. The inside pool is not bad either.
