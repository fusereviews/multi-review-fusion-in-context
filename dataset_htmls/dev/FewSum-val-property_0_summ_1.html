Summary

The restaurant is a small sandwich-and-salad type that emphasizes a more healthy style. The food is generally well-regarded, and the restaurant is clean, has a nice atmosphere, and friendly waitstaff. The restaurant is recommended for those who are interested in healthier, less mainstream ingredients such as kale or quinoa.
