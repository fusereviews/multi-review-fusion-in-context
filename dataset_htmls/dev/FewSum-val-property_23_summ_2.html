Summary

While the concept is unique and the food - when cooked correctly - can be good, the overall dining experience is highly disappointing. The service is rushed and unprofessional, the cooking area is messy and unsanitary, and building the entree bowls can be confusing. However, they do offer unlimited food options for only a few dollars extra.
